# A Few Words About Mono No Aware

**[annalinhvu.github.io/mono-no-aware](https://annalinhvu.github.io/mono-no-aware/)**

A photo-illustrated memoir I wrote for Mono No Aware's 20th anniversary exhibition — six years of showing up at a Brooklyn experimental film lab, starting as a nineteen-year-old who thought the founder was a security guard, and slowly learning to develop film, teach workshops, and become part of the community there.

It's built as one self-contained HTML page: my writing, scanned instruction sheets, texts and emails with Steve, and photos from Swim Club nights, COVID care packages, protests, workshops, and the wall we painted one afternoon. There's a light/dark toggle — "Table" for a light table, "Room" for the darkroom.

This is a personal piece, kept separate from my professional portfolio on purpose.
